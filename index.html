Hello, my name is Maarten

I'm an 22-years old digital designer and photographer from Flanders, Belgium. I love to shoot spontaneous portraits, mostly outdoors.

My photography style is eclectic; my ideas and imagery are constantly evolving on the shoot. This, combined with the ability to connect and get to the core of his subjects, allows for my images to portray the personality and the essence of each person in my portraiture.

How I work

I work most times on a time for portfolio basis, This means the photographer agrees to provide the model with a number of edited pictures of the best photographs from the session, and a limited license to use those pictures in return for the model's time. The model can use the pictures on social media platform as long as she/he credits the photographer.

Working together

If you are interested in modeling, you can send me an email or fill out the contact form in the contact section. Make sure to include some portfolio material or give me a link to a social media account. Instagram is preferred.
